About Me


Well, Hello there, beautiful people. My name is Stephanie Marie. I am a Southern California native. I have a beautiful life filled with love and a big wonderful family. Meditation is a big part of my life. I believe a healthy mind leads to a healthy life. A healthy lifestyle is very important to me. I am a huge advocate of eating clean and working out. Your diet is not only what you eat. It's what you watch, what you listen to, what you read, the people you hang around. Be mindful of the things you put into your body emotionally, spiritually, and physically. Learning daily is a must for me. I am constantly evolving because I think if you aren't evolving then you are stuck. I certainly don't pretend to know it all because true wisdom is knowing you know nothing. One can't learn what you think you already know.
